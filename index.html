About This Suite

Welcome to Universal Converter Suite! This tool allows you to easily convert units for:

  • Length (Meter, Kilometer, Mile, etc.)
  • Weight (Kg, g, lb, oz)
  • Temperature (C, F, K)
  • Volume (Liter, Gallon, m³)
  • Speed (km/h, m/s, mph)
  • Area (m², km², mi²)
  • Time (Seconds, Minutes, Hours, Days)
  • Currency (USD, QAR, PKR, EUR)
  • Energy & Pressure units
  • Data Size (Bytes, KB, MB, GB)

This suite also includes:

  • PDF → Word & Word → PDF
  • Calories & Macros Calculator
  • ✨ Dark/Light mode, responsive design, animations, and modern UI.
